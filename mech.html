Re-Engineering

Event Details.

Re Engineering relates to constructing a new part with the existing design details. With the help of this event we meet general engineering outcomes like Engineering Knowledge, Modern Tool Usage, and Team Work. The event consists of two rounds. In the First Round one of the team member will be blind folded and will select components from a box in which components related to a simple machine will be kept. The team which will assemble the components to form the given machine in least possible time will advance to the next round. In the Second Round both components and steps to assemble will be given. The team with most accurate assembly and least time will win.
Details
1-2 Members in a team

Move n Win

Event Details.

This is a technical and fun event. This event highlights communication skills and increases their confidence in public speaking The team will select random topics from a bowl like engineering, mathematics, science, sports, recreational etc to explain for a minute like a JAM. There will be steps marked on the floor. The one with most accurate explanation moves two steps ahead, a person making mistakes will move one step back. The person who crosses the last step first advances. In the second round participants will be given clues and they will have to bring a photo which corresponds to those clues, the fastest and most accurate photo wins.
Details
